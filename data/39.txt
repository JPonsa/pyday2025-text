Quick Pasta Carbonara

Classic Roman pasta dish. Ready in 20 minutes.

Ingredients:
- 400g spaghetti
- 200g guanciale or pancetta
- 4 egg yolks
- 100g Pecorino Romano
- Black pepper

Instructions:
1. Boil pasta in salted water
2. Crisp guanciale in pan, no oil needed
3. Mix yolks, cheese, pepper in bowl
4. Combine hot pasta with guanciale
5. Remove from heat, add egg mixture
6. Toss quickly, add pasta water if needed
7. Serve immediately with extra cheese

Tips: Never add cream. Real carbonara uses only eggs and cheese for creaminess. The residual heat cooks the eggs without scrambling.
